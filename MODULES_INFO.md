## 5. USERS MODULE

### Purpose
Manages user authentication, profiles, and learning progress tracking.

### User Profile Information
- **Username & Email**: Unique identifiers
- **Skills**: JSON list of technical skills (e.g., Python, JavaScript, React)
- **Interests**: JSON list of career interests
- **Experience Years**: Professional experience in tech
- **Quiz Scores**: History of assessment attempts
- **Recommended Paths**: Career suggestions based on assessments
- **Bio & Profile Image**: Personal information and avatar

## 6. LEARNING ROADMAPS & PATHS

### How to Use the Platform

1. **Take Assessments**:
   - Start with Skills Assessment to evaluate your technical knowledge
   - Complete Career Interest Assessment to discover what excites you
   - Take Learning Style Assessment to understand your preferred learning methods

2. **Review Results**:
   - Check your scores and detailed feedback
   - Understand your strengths and areas for improvement

3. **Explore Career Paths**:
   - Browse the 5 available careers
   - Review roadmaps with Basic, Intermediate, and Advanced phases
   - Check required skills and experience

4. **Get Recommendations**:
   - System provides personalized career suggestions
   - Review match scores and detailed insights
   - Access curated learning resources

5. **Practice with Games**:
   - Strengthen your coding skills
   - Earn points and build confidence
   - Progress through difficulty levels

6. **Continue Learning**:
   - Use provided reference links
   - Follow structured roadmaps
   - Track your progress regularly

## Success Metrics

**Quiz Performance**:
- Beginner: 60-75% (Foundation building)
- Intermediate: 75-85% (Growing competency)
- Advanced: 85-100% (Mastery level)

**Game Progress**:
- Beginner games: 10 points each
- Intermediate games: 15 points each
- Advanced games: 20 points each

**Career Recommendation**:
- Match score 80-100%: Excellent fit
- Match score 60-80%: Good fit
- Match score 40-60%: Possible fit with more learning
- Match score <40%: May require significant preparation
